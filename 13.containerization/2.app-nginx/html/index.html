Our Story

Founded in 1985 by the Rossi family, La Bella Italia has been serving authentic Neapolitan recipes passed down through generations. Every dish is prepared fresh daily using imported Italian produce and old-world techniques.

Our chefs trained in Naples and bring genuine flavour and warmth to every plate. Come dine with us and feel the passion in every bite.
